History Of Guisad Valley National High School

The GUISAD VALLEY NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL also known as PCNHS – Bonifacio Annex On June 13, 1968 the Baguio City Council passed and approved Resolution No. 183 -68” creating the annexes of Baguio City National High School to accommodate the growing population of high school students in the city. One of this is Bonifacio Annex.
